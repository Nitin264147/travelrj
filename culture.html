Culture of Rajasthan

Traditional Clothes

Pagri (Turban)

A symbol of honor and pride worn differently across regions.

Ghagra Choli

Colorful, embroidered attire worn by Rajasthani women.

Angrakha

A traditional upper garment for men, often worn during festivals.

Folk Dance

Ghoomar

A graceful circular dance performed by women during festivals.

Kalbeliya

A snake-like dance performed by the Kalbeliya tribe of Rajasthan.

Folk Music

Sarangi

A soulful string instrument central to Rajasthani folk melodies.

Manganiyars

Folk musicians known for devotional and love ballads.

Art & Craft

Blue Pottery

Glazed pottery known for its vibrant cobalt blue dye.

Kathputli

Traditional hand-crafted puppets used in folk storytelling.
